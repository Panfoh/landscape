Why choose InnoEnvirosh?

Our approach

We take the history and the environmental detail of the site first sometimes using GPS.
Based on the history and environmental detail gathered, we discuss our client’s dream landscape and garden and based on this make an informed decision.
Based on the informed decision, we design – bringing the conceptual idea on paper before we hit the ground.
Gives you free estimate with no added cost after finish of project.
After installation of your landscape, we have the client home fire and electrical safety training to safeguard their homes from fire.

Our Experience

InnoEnviroSH have been engaged in landscape jobs since 2013. Our clientele currently span from residential buildings to commercial properties like schools. Our regions of experience currently are the Greater Accra and the Central regions. We have been able to bring nature to the homes of our cherished customers by bringing the resources of the forest to their homes.
Our customers not only now have the luxury of a flamboyant view of nature’s beauty right in their homes but have paid for a healthier lives. Schools we have beautified with our landscape works also have benefited as these have helped increase school enrolment.

Our Values

We are committed to good practice and are passionate about what we do. Clients satisfaction is our HALLMARK.

Who We Are

InnoEnviroSH, is a dynamic and innovative firm focused on providing high quality interior and exterior landscaping installations and maintenance services while providing our client with fire and electrical safety.

Landscaping is moving from “unplanned and undersigned” and “traditional designs” to “planned and luxury” designs for many homes and sad to say many landscape have been installed without proper consideration to the history of the land and environmental detail of the site.

InnoEnviroSH has knowledgeable, experienced, committed, and disciplined men whose knowledge and experience are drawn from Forestry Research Institute of Ghana, Environmental Protection Agency, etc. to help address your landscape installation and maintenance problems. We also have personnel’s certified by NEBOSH in Health and Safety. Again we also have personnel’s experienced in the use of GIS and Auto CAD in designing.
